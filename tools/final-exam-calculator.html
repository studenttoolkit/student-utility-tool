Final Exam Marks Needed Calculator

This calculator helps students determine how much they need to score in their final exam to reach a desired overall percentage. It works for school, college, university, and competitive exams.

How This Calculator Works

The formula used is:

Required Marks = (Target − Current × (1 − Weight)) ÷ Weight

Weight is converted into decimal form before calculation.

Example

If your current score is 60%, your target is 75%, and the final exam weightage is 40%, this calculator will tell you exactly what percentage you must score in the final exam to achieve 75% overall.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this calculator accurate?
Yes. It uses a standard weighted average formula.

Can I use this for university grading?
Yes, as long as you know the weightage of your final exam.

What if the required score is above 100%?
That means reaching the target is not mathematically possible.
